📖 LEVEL 1 — THE FIVE ANGELS (79:1-5)

Surah An-Nazi'at opens with a series of cosmic oaths. Allah swears by five types of angels — revealing a vast, invisible system running every single moment.

Al-Nazi'at gharqa — the Extractors, who pull out the souls of the wicked with violent, drowning force — like iron hooks ripping through deep water. Al-Nashitat nashtan — the Releasers, who draw out righteous souls with ease, like a pearl sliding off smooth silk.

Al-Sabihat sabhan — those who float and swim freely through the heavens. Al-Sabiqat sabqan — those who race ahead at extraordinary speed. Al-Mudabbirat amran — those who administer the entire divine program of the cosmos.

Five oaths. Five categories. All pointing to one truth: there is a complete, perfect system that our eyes cannot see — yet it runs constantly, by His command.

وَالنَّازِعَاتِ غَرْقًا ۩ وَالنَّاشِطَاتِ نَشْطًا ۩ وَالسَّابِحَاتِ سَبْحًا
"By the [angels] who extract violently / and those who remove gently / and those who float through the heavens" — An-Nazi'at 79:1-3
فَالسَّابِقَاتِ سَبْقًا ۩ فَالْمُدَبِّرَاتِ أَمْرًا
"And those who race ahead / and those who administer the divine matters!" — An-Nazi'at 79:4-5

📖 LEVEL 2 — HEARTS TREMBLE (79:6-14)

The surah now takes us to the Day itself. Al-Rajifa — the first Blast of the Horn that shatters and destroys the entire universe. Al-Radifa — the second Blast that follows — raises everyone from the dead.

On that Day: "Qulub yawma'idhin wajifa" — hearts will be in violent, pounding trembling. "Absaruha khashi'a" — eyes will be downcast and humbled. There will be no arrogance left.

Yet in this world, mockers said: "Are we really going to be returned? Even after we are hollow, decayed bones?!" They thought resurrection was absurd. Allah's response: "Fa-innama hiya zajratun wahida" — it will be only a single shout — and they are all at the surface of the earth. One command. Instant resurrection.

يَوْمَ تَرْجُفُ الرَّاجِفَةُ ۩ تَتْبَعُهَا الرَّادِفَةُ ۩ قُلُوبٌ يَوْمَئِذٍ وَاجِفَةٌ ۩ أَبْصَارُهَا خَاشِعَةٌ
"On the Day the trembling one trembles and the following one follows — hearts that Day will be pounding, their eyes downcast." — 79:6-9
فَإِنَّمَا هِيَ زَجْرَةٌ وَاحِدَةٌ ۩ فَإِذَا هُم بِالسَّاهِرَةِ
"It will be only a single shout — and behold, they are at the surface of the earth." — 79:13-14

📖 LEVEL 3 — MOSES IN THE HOLY VALLEY (79:15-19)

After the cosmic picture of Judgment Day, Allah shifts to the story of Musa (Moses) alayhis-salam. It begins gently: "Hal ataka hadeeth Musa?" — "Has the story of Moses reached you?"

Moses is in Wadi Tuwa — the sacred, holy valley on the slopes of Mount Sinai. Allah calls to him personally. The mission: "Go to Pharaoh — he has transgressed all limits!"

But the message to deliver is not a threat first. It is a gentle invitation: "Hal laka ila an tazakka?" — Would you like to purify yourself? Allah sends His messenger with mercy and an offer of purification before any warning of punishment.

هَلْ أَتَاكَ حَدِيثُ مُوسَىٰ ۩ إِذْ نَادَاهُ رَبُّهُ بِالْوَادِ الْمُقَدَّسِ طُوًى
"Has the story of Moses reached you? When his Lord called to him in the sacred valley of Tuwa." — 79:15-16
اذْهَبْ إِلَىٰ فِرْعَوْنَ إِنَّهُ طَغَىٰ ۩ فَقُلْ هَل لَّكَ إِلَىٰ أَن تَزَكَّىٰ ۩ وَأَهْدِيَكَ إِلَىٰ رَبِّكَ فَتَخْشَىٰ
"Go to Pharaoh — he has transgressed! And say: Would you like to purify yourself? And I will guide you to your Lord, so you may fear Him." — 79:17-19

📖 LEVEL 4 — PHARAOH'S DEFIANCE & FALL (79:20-26)

Moses showed Pharaoh "al-aya al-kubra" — the greatest sign. The staff that became a living serpent. The hand that shone brilliantly white. Clear, undeniable evidence from Allah.

Pharaoh's response: "Fakadhdhaba wa 'asa" — he denied completely and disobeyed. "Thumma adbara yas'a" — then he turned his back and walked away hastily. Then he gathered all his people and declared: "Ana rabbukum al-A'la!" — "I AM YOUR MOST EXALTED LORD!"

The consequence: "Fa-akhadha-hu Allahu nakal al-akhirah wal-ula" — Allah seized him as a warning and punishment for both this world and the next. He drowned. His preserved body became a sign. His story, recorded for all of history.

فَأَرَاهُ الْآيَةَ الْكُبْرَىٰ ۩ فَكَذَّبَ وَعَصَىٰ ۩ ثُمَّ أَدْبَرَ يَسْعَىٰ ۩ فَحَشَرَ فَنَادَىٰ
"So [Moses] showed him the greatest sign. But he denied and disobeyed. Then he turned away hastily. Then he gathered his people and called out." — 79:20-23
فَقَالَ أَنَا رَبُّكُمُ الْأَعْلَىٰ ۩ فَأَخَذَهُ اللَّهُ نَكَالَ الْآخِرَةِ وَالْأُولَىٰ
"Saying: 'I am your most exalted lord!' — So Allah seized him with the punishment of the Last and the First." — 79:24-25

📖 LEVEL 5 — SIGNS OF CREATION (79:27-33)

Allah responds to the doubters of resurrection with a direct challenge: "A-antum ashaddu khalqan am al-sama'?" — Are you harder to create, or the entire sky?

The sky: built, raised to incomprehensible heights, perfectly proportioned. Day and night: He darkened the night completely and extracted the daylight. Both designed for your wellbeing.

The earth: "Daha-ha" — He spread it out (SubhanAllah — the word relates to an egg-shape, matching the earth's spherical form). And from it: water brought forth and pastures — every green thing, every food, for every creature. By His command alone.

أَأَنتُمْ أَشَدُّ خَلْقًا أَمِ السَّمَاءُ ۚ بَنَاهَا ۩ رَفَعَ سَمْكَهَا فَسَوَّاهَا ۩ وَأَغْطَشَ لَيْلَهَا وَأَخْرَجَ ضُحَاهَا
"Are you more difficult to create or the sky? He built it, raised its canopy and proportioned it. He darkened its night and extracted its brightness." — 79:27-29
وَالْأَرْضَ بَعْدَ ذَٰلِكَ دَحَاهَا ۩ أَخْرَجَ مِنْهَا مَاءَهَا وَمَرْعَاهَا
"And the earth after that He spread out — He brought forth from it its water and its pastures." — 79:30-31

📖 LEVEL 6 — AT-TAMMAH: THE OVERWHELMING (79:34-41)

"Al-Tamma al-Kubra" — The Greatest Catastrophe. "Tammah" from "amma" — to overwhelm, to flood, to drown out everything else. Every disaster in human history is nothing compared to this Day.

On that Day: "Yatadhaakkar al-insanu ma sa'a" — man will remember EVERYTHING he strove for. Every choice. Every priority. Every time he chose dunya over akhira. Hellfire is brought out and displayed for all to see.

Then the two paths: Path 1: Transgressed + preferred this world = Hellfire is their permanent dwelling. Path 2: Feared the standing before their Lord AND restrained their nafs from hawa (ego, desires) = Paradise is their permanent dwelling — their al-Ma'wa — their true home.

فَإِذَا جَاءَتِ الطَّامَّةُ الْكُبْرَىٰ ۩ يَوْمَ يَتَذَكَّرُ الْإِنسَانُ مَا سَعَىٰ
"When the Greatest Catastrophe comes — the Day man will remember all he strove for." — 79:34-35
وَأَمَّا مَنْ خَافَ مَقَامَ رَبِّهِ وَنَهَى النَّفْسَ عَنِ الْهَوَىٰ ۩ فَإِنَّ الْجَنَّةَ هِيَ الْمَأْوَىٰ
"And as for the one who feared standing before their Lord and restrained themselves from desire — indeed Paradise (al-ma'wa) is their refuge." — 79:40-41

📖 LEVEL 7 — THE FINAL HOUR (79:42-46)

The surah closes with the question everyone asked: "Yas'aloonaka 'an al-Sa'ah ayyan mursaha?" — They ask you about the Hour: when exactly will it arrive?

Allah's response: "Fima anta min dhikriha?" — What do you have to do with its mention? This is a gentle correction: knowing the Hour's exact timing is not within the Prophet's ﷺ knowledge. "Ila Rabbika muntahaha" — to your Lord ALONE belongs its ultimate limit.

"Innama anta mundhiru man yakhshaha" — You are ONLY a warner for whoever FEARS it. And the final, breathtaking verse: On the Day they see it, their entire life will feel like no more than an evening or its morning. 80 years reduced to the feeling of one afternoon. This world is fleeting. The Akhira is forever.

يَسْأَلُونَكَ عَنِ السَّاعَةِ أَيَّانَ مُرْسَاهَا ۩ فِيمَ أَنتَ مِن ذِكْرَاهَا ۩ إِلَىٰ رَبِّكَ مُنتَهَاهَا
"They ask you about the Hour: when is its arrival? What have you to do with its mention? To your Lord is its limit." — 79:42-44
إِنَّمَا أَنتَ مُنذِرُ مَن يَخْشَاهَا ۩ كَأَنَّهُمْ يَوْمَ يَرَوْنَهَا لَمْ يَلْبَثُوا إِلَّا عَشِيَّةً أَوْ ضُحَاهَا
"You are only a warner for whoever fears it. The Day they see it — as though they had not remained except an evening or its morning." — 79:45-46
