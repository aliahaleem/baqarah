✨ LEVEL 2 — AHAD & SAMAD (112:1-2)
🎬 Allahu Ahad — Click to reveal verse

Surah Al-Ikhlas was revealed when disbelievers demanded: "Describe your Lord to us!" — and Allah revealed four verses that shut down every false concept of God forever.

Allahu Ahad — He is ONE. Not just one in number — but Ahad means uniquely singular. No division, no peers, no comparable beings. He alone is God.

Allahu al-Samad — He is Al-Samad. This word is unique to this surah in the entire Quran. It means the one all creation depends upon, who has no needs of His own — the Perfect, Eternal Refuge.

قُلْ هُوَ اللَّهُ أَحَدٌ ۩ اللَّهُ الصَّمَدُ
"Say: He is Allah, One. Allah — the Eternal Refuge." (112:1-2)

💎 LEVEL 4 — PURE TAWHID (112:3-4)
🎬 Nothing Equal — Click to reveal verse

Al-Ikhlas is named after ikhlas (إخلاص) — which means sincerity, purity. The surah strips away every false belief about Allah until only the pure, undiluted truth remains.

The Prophet ﷺ said: "Reciting Surah Al-Ikhlas equals one-third of the Quran." Scholars explain: the Quran's themes are stories, laws, and tawhid — Al-Ikhlas IS the tawhid in concentrated form.

لَمْ يَلِدْ وَلَمْ يُولَدْ ۩ وَلَمْ يَكُن لَّهُ كُفُوًا أَحَدٌ
"He did not beget, nor was He begotten. And there is none equal to Him." (112:3-4)
