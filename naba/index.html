📖 LEVEL 1 — THE GREAT NEWS (78:1-5)

The Quraysh of Makkah heard about the Day of Resurrection and began to dispute. "Will we really be raised from the dead? Impossible!" Some mocked, some questioned, some simply refused to believe.

Allah begins this surah by asking them back: "About WHAT are they asking each other?" As if to say — don't you see? The evidence is all around you. The truth is unmistakable.

Then comes a double warning: "Kalla saya'lamoon — Thumma kalla saya'lamoon." No! They will know. Then: No! They will know. The repetition in Arabic signals absolute certainty. The Day is coming. Their denial changes nothing.

عَمَّ يَتَسَاءَلُونَ ۩ عَنِ النَّبَإِ الْعَظِيمِ
"About what are they asking one another? About the Great News." — An-Naba 78:1-2 (Sahih International)
كَلَّا سَيَعْلَمُونَ ۩ ثُمَّ كَلَّا سَيَعْلَمُونَ
"No! They are going to know. Then no! They are going to know." — An-Naba 78:4-5 (Sahih International)

After asking "about WHAT are they arguing?", Allah answers: Look at what I made for you! Earth as a resting bed. Mountains as pegs. Sleep as rest. Night as a garment. Day for work. Six signs — all pointing to the same Creator!
📖 LEVEL 2 — SIGNS OF EARTH (78:6-11)

Allah responds to the disputers with evidence: look at creation! The earth — does it not hold you steady? Does it not feel like a comfortable resting place? This is "mihad" — a smooth, stable cradle.

The mountains are called "awtad" — pegs, like tent stakes driven deep. Scientists confirm: mountains have deep roots going kilometers underground, stabilizing the earth's tectonic plates.

Your sleep is "subat" — a complete shutting down for total renewal. The night is your "libas" (garment) — it wraps you in darkness and peace. The day is your "ma'ash" — time to seek provision. Six gifts. All precisely designed for you.

أَلَمْ نَجْعَلِ الْأَرْضَ مِهَادًا ۩ وَالْجِبَالَ أَوْتَادًا
"Have We not made the earth a resting place? And the mountains as pegs?" — An-Naba 78:6-7 (Sahih International)
وَجَعَلْنَا نَوْمَكُمْ سُبَاتًا ۩ وَجَعَلْنَا اللَّيْلَ لِبَاسًا ۩ وَجَعَلْنَا النَّهَارَ مَعَاشًا
"And made your sleep as rest, the night as a covering, and the day for livelihood." — An-Naba 78:9-11 (Sahih International)

📖 LEVEL 3 — SIGNS OF SKY & RAIN (78:12-16)

Having shown the signs beneath our feet, Allah now shows the signs above our heads. Seven heavens — "shidadan" (intensely strong) — built perfectly above us. No cracks, no inconsistencies, no faults.

The sun is called "siraj wahhaj" — a blazing lamp. Functional, purposeful — not just beautiful. It gives light, warmth, and energy. The clouds — "mu'asiraat" — clouds that are squeezed and heavy with water.

Then comes the miracle chain: strong sky → blazing sun → heavy clouds → torrential rain → grain and vegetation and dense gardens. All from a single drop of water. All provision for you. "Linu'khrija bihi habban wa nabatan wa jannatin alfafa" — That We may bring forth grain, vegetation, and gardens of thick growth.

وَبَنَيْنَا فَوْقَكُمْ سَبْعًا شِدَادًا ۩ وَجَعَلْنَا سِرَاجًا وَهَّاجًا
"And built above you seven strong heavens? And made a blazing lamp?" — An-Naba 78:12-13 (Sahih International)
وَأَنزَلْنَا مِنَ الْمُعْصِرَاتِ مَاءً ثَجَّاجًا ۩ لِّنُخْرِجَ بِهِ حَبًّا وَنَبَاتًا وَجَنَّاتٍ أَلْفَافًا
"And sent down torrential rain — that We may bring forth grain, vegetation, and gardens of thick growth." — An-Naba 78:14-16 (Sahih International)

📖 LEVEL 4 — THE DAY OF SORTING (78:17-20)

Yawm al-Fasl — the Day of Decision/Sorting/Separation. "Kana miqatan" — it has an appointed time. Like a scheduled appointment — it will arrive exactly when it was written to arrive.

The angel Israfeel (AS) blows the Horn (as-Soor). At that sound, every human who ever lived rises from their graves and comes forth. They walk in vast crowds — "afwajan" — groups upon groups.

The sky tears open like curtains, becoming gates — the barrier between the seen and unseen worlds dissolves. And the mountains — those firm pegs that held the earth stable — are swept away and become a mirage. "Sarab" — you look at where they were and see nothing but empty air. The entire physical world transforms.

إِنَّ يَوْمَ الْفَصْلِ كَانَ مِيقَاتًا ۩ يَوْمَ يُنفَخُ فِي الصُّورِ فَتَأْتُونَ أَفْوَاجًا
"Indeed the Day of Decision is an appointed time — the Day the Horn is blown and you come in crowds." — An-Naba 78:17-18 (Sahih International)
وَفُتِحَتِ السَّمَاءُ فَكَانَتْ أَبْوَابًا ۩ وَسُيِّرَتِ الْجِبَالُ فَكَانَتْ سَرَابًا
"And the sky is opened and becomes gates. And the mountains are moved and become a mirage." — An-Naba 78:19-20 (Sahih International)

Ibn Ameen says:
Hellfire is described as "mirsad" — an ambush, a watch-post, lying in wait! For whom? The "tagheen" — those who transgressed all limits. No coolness there, no cold water. Only scalding hameem and dark ghassaq. Why? "They did not expect any account." May Allah protect us!
📖 LEVEL 5 — THE AMBUSH: HELLFIRE (78:21-30)

Hell is described as a "mirsad" — a watch-post, an ambush. It is not passive. It is active. Waiting. Alert. Ready for those who transgressed. "Li-l-tagheen maa'ba" — for the transgressors as a place of return.

They will remain there for "ahqaban" — ages upon ages. No escape. And what do they taste? No coolness, no refreshing drink. Only "hameem" — scalding, boiling water — and "ghassaq" — a dark, putrid, nauseating fluid. This is the opposite of Paradise's full cup.

Why this fate? Verses 27-28 explain: "They did not expect any account." They lived as if there were no Day of Judgment. They denied the signs. But Allah says: "We have enumerated ALL things in a register." Nothing was missed. Every deed was recorded.

إِنَّ جَهَنَّمَ كَانَتْ مِرْصَادًا ۩ لِّلطَّاغِينَ مَآبًا ۩ لَّابِثِينَ فِيهَا أَحْقَابًا
"Indeed Hellfire has been lying in wait — for the transgressors, a place of return. They remain in it for ages." — An-Naba 78:21-23 (Sahih International)
إِنَّهُمْ كَانُوا لَا يَرْجُونَ حِسَابًا ۩ وَكَذَّبُوا بِآيَاتِنَا كِذَّابًا
"Indeed they did not expect any account — and they denied Our signs completely." — An-Naba 78:27-28 (Sahih International)

After Hellfire comes the beautiful contrast — Paradise! "Inna lil-muttaqeena mafaza!" For the God-fearing: SUCCESS! Gardens and grape vines. Companions of equal age. A cup overflowing. And the most peaceful part: no idle talk, no lies. Pure, absolute peace. Match the rewards!
📖 LEVEL 6 — GARDENS OF PARADISE (78:31-36)

"Inna lil-muttaqeena mafaza" — For the God-fearing is "mafaz" — triumph, escape, success! This word means not just a reward, but a rescue — they escaped the terrible fate of the transgressors.

What awaits them? Hadaa'iq — enclosed, beautiful gardens. A'nab — grape vines specifically, lush and fruitful. Kawa'ib atrab — companions perfectly matched in age and beauty. Ka'san dihaqan — a cup filled to the brim, overflowing with pure drink.

And the most beautiful description: "La yasma'una fiha laghwan wa la kidhdhaba" — they will hear NO idle talk and NO falsehood in Paradise. Imagine: never hearing an argument, a lie, gossip, or a cruel word — forever. That is the peace of Jannah. A "jaza'an min rabbika" — a gift from your Lord.

إِنَّ لِلْمُتَّقِينَ مَفَازًا ۩ حَدَائِقَ وَأَعْنَابًا ۩ وَكَوَاعِبَ أَتْرَابًا ۩ وَكَأْسًا دِهَاقًا
"Indeed for the righteous is success — gardens and grape vines, companions of equal age, and a full cup." — An-Naba 78:31-34 (Sahih International)
لَّا يَسْمَعُونَ فِيهَا لَغْوًا وَلَا كِذَّابًا ۩ جَزَاءً مِّن رَّبِّكَ عَطَاءً حِسَابًا
"They will not hear therein ill speech or falsehood — a reward from your Lord, an ample gift." — An-Naba 78:35-36 (Sahih International)

📖 LEVEL 7 — STANDING BEFORE THE LORD (78:37-38)

Verse 37 establishes Who is in charge that Day: "Rabb al-samawati wal-ard wa ma baynahuma ar-Rahman" — Lord of the heavens, the earth, and all that is between them — the Most Merciful. None has authority to speak to Him without His permission.

Then comes the awe-inspiring image: "Yawma yaqoomu ar-Ruh wal-malaa'ikatu saffan" — The Day when the Spirit and the angels stand in rows. Who is "ar-Ruh"? According to many scholars including Ibn Kathir, this is Jibreel (AS) — the greatest of all angels. He fills an entire row.

On that Day of absolute silence and awe: "La yatakallamoona illa man adhina lahu ar-Rahman wa qala sawaban" — None shall speak except one whom the Most Merciful has given permission — and he will say what is correct (sawab). Perfect order. Perfect justice. Perfect mercy.

رَّبِّ السَّمَاوَاتِ وَالْأَرْضِ وَمَا بَيْنَهُمَا الرَّحْمَٰنِ ۖ لَا يَمْلِكُونَ مِنْهُ خِطَابًا
"Lord of the heavens, the earth, and whatever is between them — the Most Merciful — none will have authority to speak to Him." — An-Naba 78:37 (Sahih International)
يَوْمَ يَقُومُ الرُّوحُ وَالْمَلَائِكَةُ صَفًّا ۖ لَّا يَتَكَلَّمُونَ إِلَّا مَنْ أَذِنَ لَهُ الرَّحْمَٰنُ وَقَالَ صَوَابًا
"The Day when the Spirit and the angels stand in rows — none shall speak except one to whom the Most Merciful grants permission, and he says what is correct." — An-Naba 78:38 (Sahih International)

📖 LEVEL 8 — THE FINAL WARNING (78:39-40)

"Dhalika al-yawm al-haqq" — That is the Day of Truth. "Haqq" — the Real, the True, the Undeniable. After 38 verses of evidence (creation, signs, descriptions of the Day, Hellfire, Paradise), the surah reaches its conclusion: it's all real.

"Fa-man sha'a ittakhadha ila Rabbihi ma'aba" — So whoever wills — let him take a path back to his Lord. The door is still open. The path exists. This moment — right now — is the opportunity. "Ma'aba" means a place to return to, a path home.

Then the warning: "Inna andharnakum 'adhaban qarib" — We have warned you of a punishment that is NEAR (qarib). Not far. Not later. Near. And the final image: every person sees what their hands sent forward. And the disbeliever — overcome with regret — will say: "Ya laytani kuntu turaban!" — "I WISH I were dust!" He will wish he had never existed rather than face this Day. May Allah protect us. Ameen.

ذَٰلِكَ الْيَوْمُ الْحَقُّ ۖ فَمَن شَاءَ اتَّخَذَ إِلَىٰ رَبِّهِ مَآبًا
"That is the Day of Truth. So let whoever wills take a path back to his Lord." — An-Naba 78:39 (Sahih International)
إِنَّا أَنذَرْنَاكُمْ عَذَابًا قَرِيبًا ۖ يَوْمَ يَنظُرُ الْمَرْءُ مَا قَدَّمَتْ يَدَاهُ وَيَقُولُ الْكَافِرُ يَا لَيْتَنِي كُنتُ تُرَابًا
"We have warned you of a punishment that is near — the Day a man sees what his hands sent forth, and the disbeliever says: I wish I were dust!" — An-Naba 78:40 (Sahih International)
