# Baqarah Quest 🗺️

An interactive, Minecraft-themed learning game to help children understand the **meaning of Surah Al-Baqarah** — section by section — through animated scenes, stories, and games.

Built for children who have memorised Al-Baqarah and are ready to understand what they recite.

---

## ✨ What's Inside

| Level | Name | Verses | Game Type |
|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | 🌿 Plains of Guidance | 2:1–5 | Drag & Drop — match qualities of believers |
| 2 | 🌑 Cave of Shadows | 2:6–20 | Quiz — disbelievers, hypocrites, parables |
| 3 | 🌟 Garden of First Light | 2:21–39 | Story ordering — Adam (AS) |
| 4 | 🌊 Shores of Sinai | 2:40–61 | Drag & Drop — match the five miracles |

Each level includes:
- 🎬 **Animated canvas scene** — pixel-art with clickable characters that reveal verses
- 📜 **Story** — written for children with Ibn Ameen the Explorer as guide
- 📿 **Key Quranic verses** — Arabic text + English translation (Sahih International)
- 🎮 **Game** — must be passed to claim XP and gems and unlock the next level

---

## 🚀 How to Open

1. Open the `baqarah/` folder
2. Double-click `index.html` — it opens directly in any browser
3. **No internet required** after the first load (fonts load from Google Fonts once)
4. Progress is saved automatically in the browser (`localStorage`)

> To reset all progress: open the browser console (F12 → Console) and type `localStorage.clear(); location.reload();` — or use the **🔄 Reset** button in the top-right of the game header.

## 👨‍👦 How to Use It With Your Child

### Before the session
- Make sure your child has **recited the relevant section** of Al-Baqarah recently — even just the key verses listed in the verse card
- Sit with them for the first level — show them how to drag items, click on scenes, and claim rewards

### During each level
1. **Watch the scene together** — click on the characters and objects to reveal verse popups; read them aloud
2. **Read the story card** — Ibn Ameen explains the theme in a child-friendly way; pause and discuss
3. **Read the Arabic and English verses** — point out words your child already knows from memorisation
4. **Play the game** — let your child try independently first; only help if they're stuck after re-reading the story
5. **Claim the reward** — make a small celebration of it (high-five, du'a, etc.)

### Discussion questions to ask
- *"What does taqwa mean? Can you name one quality of the Muttaqeen?"*
- *"Why did Iblis refuse to bow? What sin was it?"*
- *"What did Adam (AS) do straight after he made a mistake? What does that teach us?"*
- *"Can you name three miracles Allah gave to Bani Isra'il in the desert?"*

## 🎮 Game Guide (for parents)

### Level 1 — Drag & Drop
Drag the quality card from the left pool and drop it onto the matching description on the right. All 5 must be correct to pass.

- **Stuck?** Re-read the story card — each quality is mentioned with its description.
- **Partial correct** (4/5): the red zone shows which one is wrong — swap that one only.

### Level 2 — Quiz
Answer 5 multiple-choice questions about disbelievers and hypocrites. Need 4/5 to pass. Wrong answers reset the quiz — re-read the story first.

### Level 3 — Story Order
Use the ↑ ↓ arrows to put 7 events from the story of Adam (AS) into the correct order. All 7 must be in the right position.

- **Tip:** read the story card from top to bottom and number the events mentally before ordering.

### Level 4 — Drag & Drop
Same as Level 1 but with 5 miracles of Bani Isra'il. The animated sea scene shows the full story (parting → soldiers → drowning → saved) — watch it fully before playing.

## 🎬 Scene Guide

Each level has an animated pixel-art scene above the game. **Click anything in the scene** to reveal the verse behind it.

| Scene | What to click |
|---|---|
| 🌿 Plains of Guidance | The praying figure, the charity figure, the Quran figure |
| 🌑 Cave of Shadows | Toggle between 3 sub-scenes: Sealed Heart, Hypocrite, Fire & Storm parables |
| 🌟 Garden of First Light | Toggle between Angels bowing / Tree scene. Click sky, angels, tree, Iblis's shadow |
| 🌊 Shores of Sinai | Click each of the 3 panels (Sea, Manna, Springs) |

The **Cave of Shadows** heart scene shows black spots appearing on the heart each time truth is rejected — this is based on the hadith in Ibn Majah about the "Ran" (stain on the heart). It's a powerful visual worth discussing with your child.
